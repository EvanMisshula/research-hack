Students: get to the edges of science

It is time to work on problems that no one has conquered. Your programming skills can help solve the mysteries of science. Those skills can also help get you into grad school. There is a severe shortage of students who can program and creae web pages that capture and display the results of science. Put your skills to use and gain recommendations and build your professsional portfolio.

Faculty: find research assistants who code

No one goes into teaching and research to talk to no one. Find students who care about science. Submit small parts of your research and compete for student research assistants with coding and web developement skills. Sponsors provide grants of in kind computing conference fees and open access fees for journals.
